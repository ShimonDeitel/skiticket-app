Privacy Policy

Skiticket is an offline-first app. All data you enter -- ticket titles, resort names, amounts, dates, and notes -- is stored locally on your device using the operating system's application storage. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party, and we do not operate any servers that receive your data.

Skiticket does not collect, sell, or share personal information. The only network activity in the app is StoreKit's built-in communication with Apple's App Store to process in-app purchases and restore transactions; we never see this data ourselves.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
